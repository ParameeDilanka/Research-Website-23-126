Literature Survey

In recent years, the global tourism industry has faced a series of significant challenges. These include the widespread impact of the COVID-19 pandemic, the socioeconomic effects of the Russia-Ukraine War, and disruptions in global supply networks. These challenges have collectively hindered the industry's potential for recovery and growth.The COVID-19 pandemic had a devastating impact on the travel and tourism sector, leading to lockdowns and emergency measures that affected businesses such as transportation and hospitality. Just as these industries began their recovery, the Russia-Ukraine conflict added further complexity and disrupted global supply chains. In addition, a global increase in inflation caused by these disruptions raised consumer prices and the cost of essential commodities, making travel planning even more challenging.Furthermore, the pandemic contributed to rising obesity rates due to factors like remote work, which led to unhealthy eating habits, decreased physical activity, increased anxiety, and disrupted sleep patterns.In response to these interconnected challenges, a holistic solution is proposed in the form of a smartphone app. This solution combines augmented reality-based incentives, health monitoring devices, and activity detection systems. These elements aim to address pandemic-related issues and broader challenges facing the tourism sector by encouraging safe exploration, monitoring health metrics, and promoting physical activity. The result is increased visitor engagement, the promotion of healthier lifestyles, and the reduction of risks associated with travel and sedentary behavior. In summary, the solution consists of a smartphone app that offers augmented reality-based rewards, monitors users' health, and provides valuable advice to help travelers stay energized.

Background

In the face of multiple challenges, the global travel and tourism industry has suffered significant setbacks. Lockdowns and emergency measures during the COVID-19 pandemic hit the industry hard, leading to widespread failures in related sectors like transportation and hospitality. Just as these businesses began their recovery journey, the Russia-Ukraine war added another layer of complexity and disrupted global supply chains. This disruption triggered a global increase in inflation, raising consumer prices and affecting essential commodities.The travel and tourism industry faced further challenges as the inflationary increase made holiday planning more difficult, resulting in a sharp decline in foreign tourists since 2019. Concurrently, obesity rates worldwide were on the rise, exacerbated by the pandemic's impact on work habits, leading to unhealthy eating, decreased physical activity, heightened anxiety, and disrupted sleep patterns.In response to these interconnected challenges, a comprehensive research strategy is proposed. It comprises four crucial components: an augmented reality-based incentive system, a health monitoring device, and an activity detection system. Using deep learning and wearable technologies, the activity detection system monitors and encourages physical activity while discouraging sedentary behavior. The health monitoring technology provides real-time assessments of hydration and calorie consumption, tracking calorie and hydration levels. The augmented reality incentive system promotes safe exploration of challenging terrains, guiding users to safer paths and rewarding their achievements.This innovative approach combines the "move-to-earn" concept with cutting-edge mobile application capabilities to offer a holistic strategy for revitalizing the tourism industry. It aims to address current sector challenges by offering personalized travel options, promoting physical activity, monitoring health indicators, and encouraging safe exploration. The study's findings have potential implications for boosting tourism, promoting healthier lifestyles, and reducing risks associated with travel and inactivity. At its core, the service includes a smartphone app that provides augmented reality-based rewards, monitors users' health, and offers valuable advice to keep travelers energized during their journeys.

Research Gap

The research project described here describes the creation of a smartphone application that combines activity identification, health monitoring, and a rewards system to encourage exercise and hiking. Despite the concept's promise, there remains a substantial research vacuum in the body of knowledge. Only a small number of studies have looked at the thorough integration of these elements in a single mobile application, particularly with an emphasis on promoting outdoor activities like hiking. Additionally, the application of machine learning models for activity detection, hydration tracking via wearable IoT devices, and calorie estimation based on picture recognition provides a unique method that calls for additional research. By coming up with a novel approach to encourage healthy lives and outdoor exploration, our research hopes to close these disparities.

Research Problem

Offering adjustable prizes or personalized awards catered to specific user preferences and behaviors: Which is the most efficient strategy for encouraging physical activity within a mobile application?Using wearable sensors and machine learning algorithms, what method is the best for activity recognition. How can the numerous activities users participate in be effectively identified and categorized by these algorithms to increase the value of the mobile application.How can an IoT wearable gadget be created for travel that smoothly accompanies travelers without becoming a hassle, and how can the device be made to properly estimate hydration levels and calories burnt while travelling?Our thesis is centered on these research issues, which seek to address important facets of creating a thorough mobile application for increasing physical activity and improving the entire travel experience. In order to advance the domains of technology, health, and tourism, the research will examine the efficacy of incentive, the optimization of activity recognition techniques, and the creation of user-friendly IoT gadgets.


Objectives

  • Main Objective

    Main objective of this research project is to promote tourism industry from the uniqueness and attraction given by the people to the travel destinations and combining move-to-earn concept with the hiking destinations by giving rewards to the users and enhance the industry by supporting both users and the people who are directly and indirectly depend on the tourism industry. With combination of activity recognition and health monitoring users can use this app for health purposes also.

  • Specific Objective - Activity Recognition System

    One of the main goals of this research project is to create a cutting-edge mobile application that integrates social media and activity identification. The main objective is to encourage and urge consumers to partake in vigorous physical activity. Several crucial stages have been discovered in order to do this. The project's first step is to discover and define pertinent physical activity behaviors that are helpful in enhancing users' general health and wellbeing.The research then concentrates on choosing appropriate sensor data and putting machine learning methods into practice. The goal is to provide precise activity recognition, which will improve the application's efficiency in monitoring and promoting physical activity.Additionally, a sample of individuals who have been comparatively inactive for a protracted amount of time will be gathered for this study. The intervention's target demographic will be this user group. The information gathered from these users will be crucial in determining if the mobile application has a positive effect on raising physical activity levels.Furthermore, the user interface and user experience design for the mobile application are of utmost significance. The software should be interesting and simple to use, giving consumers a smooth and delightful experience. The project intends to further encourage consumers to embrace physical exercise as a part of their daily routine by designing an engaging interface.

  • Specific Objective - Health Monitoring System

    The creation of a health monitoring system, particularly in the form of a wearable gadget, is another essential component of this research endeavor. The goal is to create a wearable that can quickly and effectively identify user health characteristics and give consumers real-time information about their health, especially while they are on the go.Heart rate and water levels will be the two primary health indicators that the health monitoring system will concentrate on identifying. These crucial health measures will be continually monitored by the wearable device thanks to the incorporation of sensors and processing of data obtained from these sensors.The device will also determine whether a user is adequately hydrated and whether their heart rate is within the typical range. The system will instantly alert the user with pertinent voice messages if deviations from the usual are found and last for a long period of time. This proactive approach to health monitoring places an emphasis on staying hydrated and maintaining a healthy heart rate in order to safeguard the wellbeing of travelers while they are travelling.

  • Specific Objective - Rewards System

    A crucial part of this study is the rewarding system, which is intended to recognize significant junctures in hiking journeys and offer hikers rewards . In order to precisely identify these milestone markers, machine learning techniques will be used. The technology will not only recognize these markings but also handle any issues or suggestions made by hikers, guaranteeing a pleasurable experience for users.This system will improve the overall hiking experience by utilizing augmented reality (AR) technology and rewarding users when they reach particular path milestones. This hiking-related feature of gaming is anticipated to inspire more people to participate in outdoor activities and experience the great outdoors while relishing the gratification of accomplishing noteworthy goals.The Activity Recognition System, Health Monitoring System, and Rewarding System are three interrelated parts that work together to provide a full mobile application. This software seeks to motivate users to adopt a healthy lifestyle, keep them informed about their health when travelling, and enhance their hiking adventures. The research makes an effort to achieve these goals by combining technology innovation with user centered design

Solution

Activity Recognition Syatem

In order to address the research problem at hand, we have developed a comprehensive solutions. This solution encompasses several key steps aimed at improving user engagement and promoting healthier lifestyles through technology-driven interventions.Our approach begins with the collection of diverse activity data, which forms the foundation of our study. This data includes information such as age, gender, weather conditions, location changes, time-related factors, and user preferences for nearby location suggestions. To ensure data accuracy, we implement a rigorous preprocessing stage, enhancing the quality of the dataset for superior output compared to existing systems.We build a predictive machine learning model based on a classification framework. This model serves as the core engine of our solution, allowing us to make personalized recommendations and deliver user-specific feedback.Our solution incorporates insights derived from both physical activity monitoring and social media interaction patterns. Users receive personalized feedback that helps them make informed choices about their daily activities. Furthermore, we provide recommendations for nearby outdoor activities, encouraging users to engage in healthier pursuits.To ensure user-friendliness and real-time updates, our mobile app seamlessly integrates with wearable devices and social media APIs. This integration allows users to track their progress and receive notifications about their social media usage and periods of inactivity.Throughout this process, we prioritize ethical considerations and user privacy as paramount concerns. We take measures to safeguard user data and ensure that their information remains confidential and secure.Our solution includes a robust evaluation strategy that focuses on key aspects such as user engagement, changes in activity levels, and self-reported improvements in well-being. This ongoing evaluation process enables us to continuously refine our approach and enhance its effectiveness.In summary, our solutionleverages technology to motivate users toward adopting healthier lifestyles by establishing meaningful connections between their daily activities and choices that contribute to overall wellness.

Rewarding System

a system where users input location details via a mobile app for hiking destinations. The study examines the influence of these inputs on rewards distributed by a machine learning model trained with algorithms like Light GBM, CatBoost, and XGBoost. Users become eligible for rewards when they successfully reach specific destinations predicted as rewardable by the model. The app includes 100 rewardable places, offering rewards across these locations. By actively involving users in the hiking experience through inputting geographical parameters, the system aims to provide personalized rewards based on the predicted suitability of each destination. This integration of location-based rewards introduces gamification and motivation to the hiking journey, encouraging users to explore diverse places and achieve personal milestones. The system combines geolocation technology, machine learning algorithms, and reward mechanisms to enhance the hiking experience. By leveraging the impact of input values on rewards, the model aims to give users a sense of accomplishment and satisfaction upon reaching rewardable destinations. The inclusion of 100 predefined rewards ensures an engaging and varied experience, motivating users to explore a wide range of locations accessible through the mobile app.

Health Monitoring System

This health monitoring system is primarily intended to track the user's calorie intake and level of hydration. The primary aim of the Trek is to increase people's interest in travel. Therefore, it's crucial to learn about their health while they on their journey. In order to meet that demand, we intended to forecast and notify users via voice messages and alerts if their calorie intake is insufficient and whether their hydration level is low before the situation becomes serious. The data is gathered using a specially constructed IOT wearable that has heart rate, SPO2, and Android sensors.
